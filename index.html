Our Story

APEX was founded with a simple ambition — to bring together design creativity and surveying rigour in a way the industry had never quite seen. Starting with residential projects in London, we quickly grew our reputation for thoroughness and elegance. Today we work across residential, commercial, and mixed-use sectors, always with the same founding principle: do it right, first time.

Our Approach

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all. Every project begins with listening — understanding your goals, constraints, and vision. From there, our consultants craft bespoke strategies drawing on deep technical knowledge and creative problem-solving. The result is always a solution that's practical, beautiful, and built to last.

Qualifications

Our team holds memberships with RICS, RIBA, and other leading professional bodies. All surveys and design work conform to current British Standards and regulatory requirements. We carry full professional indemnity and public liability insurance on every project — giving you complete peace of mind.

Our Reach

Based in London and operating across the South East and nationwide for select projects, we're positioned to serve clients wherever they need us. Our network of trusted specialist consultants means no project is too complex or too niche for the APEX team.
